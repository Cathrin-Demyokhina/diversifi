About Us

Diversifi is the first tech-driven crypto asset management platform.

The strategies and technology behind our instruments make crypto more accessible, long-term and ESG compliant.

We serve as a bridge between traditional finance and the disruptive crypto world, and firmly believe that the effective and sustainable future of finance lies in the symbiosis of the two.

Our customers include investment banks, funds, brokers, family offices, and corporations holding crypto treasuries.
